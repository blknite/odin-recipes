Scrambled eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are an american breakfast staple that can be as simple or as fancy as you want it to be. Today we'll be going over a basic scrambled eggs recipe but with a little flair to make it look more appetizing.

Ingredients

  • Depending on serving size, as many eggs you would like to make. In this case: 4 eggs.
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 2-4 chopped chives
  • Ketchup or hot sauce of choice (optional)
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • Milk or water

Steps

  1. First beat the eggs. Place them in a medium bowl, and whisk until the yolk and whites are thoroughly combined. Add milk or water and whisk again.
  2. Next, gently preheat the pan. Melt a little butter inside it. Warm the skillet over medium heat.

  3. Finally, cook. Pour in the egg mixture, and let it cook for a few seconds, undisturbed. Then, pull a rubber spatula across the bottom of the pan to form large, soft curds of scrambled eggs.

    Continue cooking over medium-low heat, folding and stirring the eggs every few seconds. As you work, make sure to scrape your spatula along the bottom and sides of the pan to continue to form curds and to prevent any part of the eggs from drying out.

    For a soft, creamy scramble, stop when the eggs are mostly set, but a little liquid egg remains. Remove the pan from the heat, and season to taste with salt, pepper, chives, and sauce of choice.
