Tiktok Ramen

Description

Tikok ramen is an exciting new way to make ramen that uses common ingredients found in any pantry, and for those that do not love the high sodium broth found in most grocery store ramen brands.

Paired with well cooked meat this can be a great low-calorie/high protein meal that fits into weight loss diets!

Ingredients

  • Any ramen noodle uncooked (for this we will use top-ramen).
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red chili pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp light brown sugar
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Chopped scallions

Steps

  1. Start off by adding the uncooked ramen noodles to a boiling pot of water and cook until al-dente. Remove cooked noodles.
  2. Add butter to a pan on low-medium heat.
  3. Once butter is fully melted, add minced garlic and crushed red chili pepper. Cooke and stir for 30 seconds or until aromatic.
  4. Add soy sauce and brown sugar and stir until sugar is melted.
  5. Add in ramen noodles and stir fry until sauce is fully covering the noodles.
  6. Move noodles to the side and add in beaten eggs, fold eggs onto itself for a miniature omelette.
  7. Once eggs are cooked add noodles and eggs to a plate, sprinkle on sesame seeds, chopped scallions and black pepper.
  8. (Optional) Add in cooked meat of choice.
